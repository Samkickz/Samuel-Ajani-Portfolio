This task focuses on the design and implementation of a Business Intelligence (BI) solution for an e-commerce dataset using Microsoft Power BI. 
The report details the analysis of sales and marketing data from an e-commerce company, including key metrics like revenue, cost of goods, net profit, and average order value. 
It involves data preprocessing, cleansing, and modeling, and discusses findings based on the analysis of sales revenue, product popularity, customer behavior, and gender-based purchasing trends. 
The report concludes with recommendations for improving data accuracy and analysis.
